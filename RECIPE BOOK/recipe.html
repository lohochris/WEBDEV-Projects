Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Ground Beef 100g
Tomato Sauce 1 cup
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive Oil 2 tbsp

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 2

Steps

1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.

2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.

3. Add ground beef, cook until browned, then pour in the tomato sauce and simmer.

4. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and enjoy!

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 3/4 cup
Egg 1 large
Butter (melted) 2 tbsp
Sugar 1 tbsp

Details

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 4

Steps

1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.

2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.

3. Pour batter onto a hot griddle and cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Grilled Lemon Chicken

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken Breast 2 pieces
Lemon Juice 1/4 cup
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Salt and Pepper To taste

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 2

Steps

1. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.

2. Marinate chicken breasts in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.

3. Grill the chicken over medium heat until cooked through and golden brown.
